STORY

Kasama in Tagalog means "together". This world's first Michelin-star modern Filipino
restaurant is a bakery from chefs Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores, located in the East
Ukrainian Village neighborhood of Chicago. At Kasama restaurant, we offer more than just food,
it's all about tons of flavors and feelings of comfort and appreciation.

We provide a unique experience that brings together
the rich tapestry of Filipino culture, flavors, and traditions.
